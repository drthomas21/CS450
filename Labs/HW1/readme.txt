Compile
-------
Needs at least C++11 compiler
Needs to be compiled on a Windows environment

Configuration
-------------
You can modify the constants in "lib.h" to enable/disable a few debugging options and to enable bullet tracing

Shooting
--------
You can shoot only one bullet at a time. Meaning that you cannot fire another bullet until the bullet hits the ground or hits the target.
When the bullet hits the target, the target's direction and speed will change, depending on how the bullet hits the target
